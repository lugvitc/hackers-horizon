What is the Venue of the Event?
MG Auditorium, VIT Chennai
When is the event happening?
11th and 12th of February, 2023.
Will the event go on continuously for 2 days?
No, the event is divided into 2 days and is to be hosted from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM on both days.
Is the event open to non-VITians and outer professionals?
Yes, the event is open to all students and professionals.
Will accommodation be provided for non-VITians and outer professionals?
Yes, accommodation will be provided for outer professionals only on 11th February (after 7 PM) in the hostels based on availability.
Can we participate online?
No, the event is offline. We will not stream the workshop online.
What facilities do the participants get?
There will be entry passes made for each participant and refreshments will be given during the evening. A fast Wi-Fi connection will be provided for all the participants to follow along the workshop.
